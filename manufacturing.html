FAQ

Common questions

Do you specialise in PVC/uPVC pipe manufacturing?

Yes. Alongside general SME manufacturing, PVC/uPVC pipes and fittings is a core specialisation — informed by 4 years working hands-on inside uPVC manufacturing and marketing.

What's usually the fastest win?

Cost-out — typically raw-material procurement, scrap reduction and inventory. We set a baseline and target the biggest, quickest margin impact first.

Can you help with dealers and sales too?

Yes. We connect the factory to the market — dealer-network development, field campaigns and bilingual price lists — so capacity turns into sales.

What size of manufacturer do you work with?

Mostly SME and mid-sized units. If you run a plant and want lower cost, smoother production or wider distribution, the size of your team doesn't matter.
