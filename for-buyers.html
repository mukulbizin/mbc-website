FAQ

Common questions

How do I start?

Submit a procurement request with your specifications and budget. The MBC Procurement Desk takes it from there and sources on your behalf.

Will I see supplier contact details?

No. In a managed network you never contact suppliers directly. MBC handles all communication, quotes and negotiation, and reports back to you.

What does it cost?

Submitting requests is free. MBC is paid mainly from the savings it delivers — a success fee on verified savings (no savings, no success fee).

How do I track progress?

Your Buyer Area shows each request moving through Submitted → Sourcing → Quotes ready → Closed, with savings reported on completion.
