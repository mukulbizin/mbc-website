FAQ

Common questions

Do I need technical knowledge or a big budget?

No. We use low-cost, no-code tools and handle setup. Most SME automations start small and pay back within a few months.

Will automation replace my staff?

No — it removes boring, repetitive work so your people focus on selling and serving customers.

Which tools do you use?

WhatsApp Business, Google Sheets/Forms, a free or low-cost CRM, simple automation, and AI assistants like ChatGPT and Claude. Paid tools only when ROI is clear.

Yes. We set up a simple, safe AI-use policy and handle your data with care from day one.
