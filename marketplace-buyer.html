FAQ

Common questions

How do I start sourcing?

Submit a buyer enquiry below or message us on WhatsApp. MBC reviews your requirement and matches you with verified suppliers — your identity stays private.

Do I pay to post an enquiry?

No. Posting enquiries is free. MBC's fee applies only on successful sourcing, usually as a share of the savings we deliver.

Will suppliers see my company name?

No. Suppliers cannot see buyer details. All communication is routed through MBC until you choose to proceed.

How is the savings fee calculated?

We agree a clean baseline first, then our fee is a share of the verified Year-1 savings (typically 10–25%). No savings, no success fee.
