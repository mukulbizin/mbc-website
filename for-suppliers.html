FAQ

Common questions

How do I get business through MBC?

Register and get verified, list your products/services and quotations, then respond to procurement requests that MBC assigns to you.

Will I see buyer contact details?

No. In a managed network you never contact buyers directly. MBC routes every request, quote and negotiation, and protects buyer identity.

Does it cost to list?

Listing is free for verified suppliers. Featured placement and any success-based terms apply as separately agreed.

How are requests assigned?

The MBC Procurement Desk matches buyer requirements to relevant verified suppliers and invites quotes — you respond through MBC.
