How a request moves through the Desk

1

Buyer submits

A procurement request with specs and budget arrives at the Desk.

2

Desk matches

MBC selects verified suppliers and invites quotes — identities masked.

3

Suppliers quote

Quotes return to the Desk; suppliers never see the buyer.

4

Desk negotiates

MBC negotiates the best price and terms on the buyer's behalf.

5

MBC issues a savings & success-fee report; the buyer pays from the savings.

FAQ

Common questions

What is the MBC Procurement Desk?

The managed middle of the network. It receives every request, matches buyers with suppliers, negotiates pricing, calculates savings, generates success-fee reports, and controls all communication.

Why route everything through MBC?

It protects both sides' privacy, keeps sourcing professional and competitive, and lets MBC negotiate harder on the buyer's behalf — aligning everyone's incentives.

How are savings and fees calculated?

Against an agreed baseline. Savings = baseline − achieved price (like-for-like); MBC's fee is a share of verified savings. No savings, no success fee.
