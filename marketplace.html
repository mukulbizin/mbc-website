Pay from what you save

Savings-based & success-fee pricing

For buyers, MBC can work on a success/savings-based model: we benchmark a clean baseline, source and negotiate on your behalf, and our fee is a share of the savings we actually deliver (typically 10–25% of Year-1 savings). If we don't save you money, you don't pay a success fee.

ModelHow it worksBest for
Savings-basedFee = a share of verified savings vs your baselineBuyers with recurring or large spend
Success feeFee on a successful, completed sourcing dealOne-off or project purchases

FAQ

Common questions

Is this an open marketplace?

No. It is a managed procurement network. Buyers and suppliers never contact each other directly — MBC vets every listing and routes all communication, quotations, negotiation and sourcing.

Can I contact a supplier directly?

No. All enquiries, quotes and negotiation are handled by MBC on your behalf. This protects both sides' privacy and keeps sourcing professional and confidential.

What does it cost to use?

Browsing and posting enquiries is free for buyers. MBC earns primarily through a success/savings-based fee on completed sourcing — see the Buyer Portal.

Who can list as a supplier?

Verified manufacturers, distributors, importers and service providers. Every listing is reviewed and approved by MBC before publication.
