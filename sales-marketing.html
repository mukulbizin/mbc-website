FAQ

Common questions

Do you run my ads and social media?

We build the system that makes marketing work — lead capture, follow-up, campaigns and materials — and set up channels (especially WhatsApp and local listings) so your team can run them. Everything ties back to enquiries and sales.

What's the first thing you'd fix?

Usually follow-up and speed-to-lead. Most SMEs lose more sales to slow or missing follow-up than to a shortage of leads.

Will this work for B2B and dealer-led businesses?

Yes — that's our core, built from real distribution and dealer-network experience, not generic B2C theory.

Do you provide bilingual materials?

Yes. Flyers, price lists, catalogues and campaigns can be prepared in English and Bengali.
