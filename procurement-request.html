FAQ

Common questions

What is a procurement request?

It's a sourcing request (RFQ). You tell MBC exactly what you need; we find and negotiate with verified suppliers on your behalf, confidentially.

Is it free to post a request?

Yes. Posting a procurement request is free. MBC's fee applies only on successful sourcing, usually as a share of the savings delivered.

Will my request be public?

No. Your request goes only to MBC. Suppliers never see your identity; we approach them on your behalf.
