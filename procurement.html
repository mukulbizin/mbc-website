FAQ

Common questions

How quickly will we see savings?

Most engagements surface quick wins within the first few weeks of the spend analysis, while larger structural savings build over the roadmap.

How are fees structured?

Fixed-fee for defined scope, or a hybrid base fee plus a share of Year-1 savings (typically 10–25%). The first consultation is free and you approve scope and fee before work begins.

Will this disrupt our current suppliers?

No. We protect supply continuity — vendor rationalisation and renegotiation are done methodically, keeping reliable partners and improving terms.

What size of business do you work with?

Mostly SMEs and mid-market manufacturers, distributors and traders. If you have meaningful annual spend, there's usually a worthwhile saving to capture.
