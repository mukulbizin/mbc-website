FAQ

Common questions

Is there a fee to list?

Listing is currently free for verified suppliers. MBC may charge for featured placement or on successful deals — we'll always be clear up front.

How long does approval take?

MBC reviews each supplier, product and service before publication, usually within a few working days, to keep the marketplace trusted.

Will buyers contact me directly?

No. Buyer enquiries come through MBC. You won't see buyer contact details, and buyers won't see yours, until both sides agree to proceed.

What can I list?

Products and services across manufacturing, trading, agri-inputs, electronics, plumbing, packaging, logistics and more — subject to MBC approval.
