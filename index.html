FAQ

Common questions

How do fees work?

The first consultation is always free. After that, fees depend on scope — one-time projects are quoted upfront and ongoing support works monthly. You always know the cost before any work begins.

Which industries do you work with?

Manufacturing (incl. PVC/uPVC), procurement & supply chain, healthcare, agriculture & agri-business, electronics & mobile accessories, dry fruits, plumbing & sanitary, and EdTech. Financial modelling and AI automation apply across all of them.

Do you really offer AI automation for small businesses?

Yes — practical, low-cost, no-code-first automation: WhatsApp lead capture and follow-ups, dashboards, and AI assistants for quotes and content. Most automations pay back within 1–6 months.

Which languages do you work in?

English, Bengali and Hindi. All documents can be prepared bilingually in English and Bengali.
