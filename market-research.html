FAQ

Common questions

Is this affordable for an SME?

Yes — research is scoped to the decision and your budget. We focus on the few facts that actually change the call.

Do you do primary research too?

We combine desk research and market data with targeted primary inputs (interviews/surveys) where the decision needs them.

How is this different from a big agency study?

It's practical and action-ready — sized for an owner-led business, ending in a clear go/no-go and next steps, not a 200-page report.

Can it support a loan or investor pitch?

Yes — a credible market and feasibility view strengthens your case with banks and investors.
